Pad Thai

"Pad Thai is a beloved Thai street food dish featuring stir-fried rice noodles with shrimp or chicken, tofu, peanuts, and bean sprouts, all tossed in a tangy tamarind sauce."

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles (200g)
  • Shrimp or chicken (200g)
  • Eggs (2), lightly beaten
  • Garlic cloves (2-3), minced
  • Red chili flakes (1/2 tsp, optional)
  • Roasted peanuts (1/4 cup), chopped
  • Vegetable oil (2-3 tbsp)
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Tamarind sauce.

Steps

  1. Prepare Noodles and Ingredients.
  2. Cook Protein and Eggs.
  3. Stir-fry Noodles.
  4. Combine and Season.
